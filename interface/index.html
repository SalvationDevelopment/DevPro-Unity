The Basics

DevPro is a suite of opensource web and desktop applications that allow you to play the Yu-Gi-Oh! TCG/OCG/Anime card game online with people around the world. The suite uses YGOPro as the base client along with a special launcher system for chat, customization, and file management.

Play in the browser or on the desktop, this is a system designed from user request and hard work that spares no excuse.

Massively Multiplayer

DevPro Online is the second largest sim in the YGOPro Network serving all countries outside of Asia. With such an vast coverage area DevPro Online rarely drops below 900 connected users, and around peak hours reaches over 3100. But that is just our little part of the YGOPro Network, we intergrate to the International Checkmate Server accessing users from Dawn of a New Era, Percival18, My-Card, and local Checkmate duelist. This creates a stable international area of over 800 active duels going on at any given time.

Automatic Rulings & Gameplay

YGOPro is the heart of DevPro Online, the simulator uses Lua to apply known rules 100% via server scripts. There is no cheating on YGOPro Clients. Via an international team of Judges, Programmers, and bug reports we maintain ruling intergrity.

Self Updates & Maintaince

DevPro Online automatically updates its launcher and client software on startup, onced installed and online you will always have the latest announced cards, rulings, software, features, and options.
